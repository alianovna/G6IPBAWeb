What is
Generative
Artificial
Intelligence

Generative AI has emerged as a
groundbreaking technology that has
significantly advanced the capabilities of AI
systems, going beyond the limitations of
traditional AI. In this study, we aim to explore
how generative AI has revolutionized various
aspects of technology, including image
generation, website code creation, and data
output that closely resembles human-created
content. Traditional AI, often referred to as rule-based
or expert system AI, relies on a set of
predefined rules and structured data to make
decisions and provide outputs. Generative AI
leverages deep learning techniques, such as
neural networks, to learn patterns and
features from vast datasets and then
generate content that exhibits human-like
characteristics.

SIGNIFICANCE OF THE STUDY

The significance of this study lies in the transformative impact of Artificial Intelligence
on education. While AI Technologies offer efficiency and accessibility, there are
concerns about potential job displacement for educators and a reduction in critical
thinking skills among students. Additionally, there is a risk of diminished human
interaction and social development in educational settings. This study seeks to explore
these implications and promote responsible AI usage, emphasizing that AI should
enhance education rather than replace teachers and human interactions.
